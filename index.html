Educational Disparity
There is a massive disparity in our educational system that puts many at a severe disadvantage
What is it?
Any socially excluded communities that have limited academic resources such as:
  • School Funding
  • Teachers
  • Books
  • Technology
    • Why is it bad?
    Everybody loses when people don't get the education they deserve
    Can lead to:
  • Income Inequality
  • Higher Crime Rates
  • Increased Prison Population
  • Homelesness
  • Unemployment
    • Who gets hurt?
    The highest percentage of people hurt by educational disparity are African Americans and Latino Americans
    Gender is also a factor too!
    What can you do?
    VOTE!
    The first thing somebody can do is their due diligence and vote for the best representatives to solve these issues
    The next thing to do is spread awareness about the issue!
